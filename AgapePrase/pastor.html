Pastor Matthew Reyes

Pastor Matt is the Senior Pastor of Vancouver Filipino SDA Church and Abundant Life Filipino- Canadian SDA Church. He has been actively and faithfully serving the Seventh-day Adventist Church since 2000 - six years in the Philippines, four years in Micronesia, and Kootenay, British Columbia for 3 years. He graduated from the Adventist University of the Philippines with two degrees: Bachelor of Arts in Theology and Bachelor of Arts in History and Philosophy of Religion.
