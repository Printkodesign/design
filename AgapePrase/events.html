Valentines Music Dinner

This is a perfect “date night” concert that feature members of the Church's Music Ministry and the Youth Band. You will be serenaded with your favorite love songs. Concertgoers will enjoy delicious champagne and chocolates, which were served after the concert.

Annual Camping

For 25 years, The BC Adventist Community invites you to join us to this fun filled outdoor camping. Many members from all over BC will gather to this annual event on July 2-9 2023.

Some members of the Agape Praise Church are blessed to have an RV. Its going to be memorable trip. The group will visit some famous places around the area like the Canon Beach.
